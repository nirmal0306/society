About My Gate

My Gate is a smart society management system designed to simplify communication, security, and maintenance operations for modern residential communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to digitize residential society management by providing a secure, transparent, and easy-to-use platform for residents, security staff, and administrators.

Our Vision

We aim to build safer and smarter communities where residents can manage complaints, visitors, maintenance payments, and society updates — all in one place.

What We Offer

Visitor Management

Approve or reject visitors instantly for enhanced security.

Complaint Tracking

Raise, track, and resolve complaints efficiently.

Online Maintenance

Secure digital payment system for maintenance fees.

Events & Notices

Stay updated with society announcements and activities.
